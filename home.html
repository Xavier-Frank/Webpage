Who Am I?

Well, this question erupts different thoughts in my mind and gives a whole new point of view [~perspective]; to my life each time I think of it. In brief, the best perspective that I like using and that which outweighs the others is that I am an enthusiast of technology and a problem solver. With our daily lives and the world consequently being revolutionized by technology, I have always had that burning desire to be part of the revolution.

Come to think of it, with technology changing every aspect of our lives from the way we pay bills, transfer money, communicate, travel(space exploration) and even how we Date just to mention a few; one can only wonder at the extent of technology and what it can do to humanity. As technology continues to grow it is important to remain "afloat" by getting up to date with the relevant information to remain relevant in this competitive world; as Alan Kay(a Computer Scientist) once said: "Technology is anything that wasn't around when you were born".

Technological advancement has seen various sectors of our economy gradually growing and increasing in diversity. For instance, our education system has benefited from online learning which has impacted learners with extra knowledge they could not acquire from their classrooms. It is this growth and diversity in different sectors of an economy that has seen some nations develop into Super Powers. Technology has also come with its cons from internet addictions which has lead to time wastage and destruction from work, air, and water pollution from factories, Cyber thefts among others.

Since arguably the prons in technology outweigh the cons, technology is here to stay and it will undoubtedly continue to grow and revolutionize humanity from time to time. It is therefor the high time we embrace technology and use it to our advantage.
